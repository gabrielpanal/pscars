WHO ARE WE?

PSCars is a car trading company in the Philippines that buys and sells pre-owned luxury cars. We believe all of us have their dream car. But most of these cars are out of our financial capacity. Here at PSCars, we aim to make luxury cars affordable by acquiring pre-owned ones.
